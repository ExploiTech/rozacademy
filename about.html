Roz Academy

Roz Academy is a faith-based, purpose-driven school established in 2015 by Apostle J.A. Adeleke under divine direction. Located in the heart of Abuja, Nigeria, the Academy was founded to raise a generation of learners who are academically excellent, morally upright, and spiritually grounded. With a passionate commitment to holistic education, Roz Academy provides a nurturing environment where children are inspired to grow intellectually, emotionally, socially, and spiritually. Our mission is to develop students into confident leaders who will make a positive impact in their communities and the world at large. Guided by strong Christian values and a dedication to quality teaching, our team of experienced educators supports each child’s unique potential. We blend sound academics with life skills, creativity, discipline, and spiritual development to raise well-rounded individuals. Roz Academy is more than a school—it is a family where every child is seen, known, and empowered to succeed.
