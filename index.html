Roz Academy

Roz Academy was founded in 2015 by Apostle J.A. Adeleke, under divine inspiration during his service in the Empowerment Prayer Ministry.
The Lord laid it upon his heart to establish a school that would raise and nurture future leaders.
Despite many challenges, Apostle Adeleke obeyed the call, and Roz Academy was birthed.

With strong support from the Proprietress, Mrs. Feyisayo Suzzy Adeleke, the school began operations at Kobi Makaranta, Asokoro Extension, Abuja.
The school opened its doors to its first set of learners — about 17 pupils — including Mary Adewumi, who was among the pioneering students in the Primary section.

Since its inception, Roz Academy has been committed to academic excellence, character building, and spiritual growth. Over the years,
the school has proudly raised outstanding students who have gone on to become university scholars, skilled professionals, entrepreneurs, and responsible citizens. Notable alumni include Caleb Nwachukwu, Saviour Lambat, Wayo and many others.

Roz Academy continues to stand as a beacon of hope and transformation, raising generations of purposeful and God-fearing leaders.

Why Choose Roz Academy?

At Roz Academy, we are committed to raising a generation of academically sound, morally upright, and spiritually grounded leaders. Here’s why families in Abuja and beyond choose us:
1. Faith-Based Foundation: Founded on divine instruction and rooted in Christian values, Roz Academy nurtures learners in a spiritually enriching environment that builds character and purpose.
2. Academic Excellence: Our curriculum blends the Nigerian national curriculum with global best practices, ensuring students are well-prepared for higher education and life beyond the classroom.
3. Experienced & Passionate Educators: We are blessed with a team of dedicated teachers who are not only qualified but also passionate about teaching, mentoring, and inspiring each learner.
4. Small Class Sizes for Personalized Learning: With our manageable class sizes, every child receives the attention and support they need to thrive academically and personally.
5. Holistic Development: Roz Academy focuses on the all-round development of each child—academics, creativity, leadership, moral values, and life skills.
6. Proven Track Record: We have proudly raised students who have gone on to become university scholars, entrepreneurs, skilled professionals, and value-driven citizens.
7. Strong Moral & Disciplinary Standards: We instill discipline, respect, and integrity in our students—principles that shape their lives far beyond their time at Roz Academy.
